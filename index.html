Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Radhika Kapoor, Pinch of Pink began as a small studio focused on one-on-one custom outfits. What started as a passion project for friends and family soon grew into a boutique name for exclusive cloth work and thoughtful detailing.

Today, we continue to work in small batches ‚Äî prioritising fit, fabric, and finish over mass production. Every pattern is refined, every stitch inspected and every piece finished by hand.
